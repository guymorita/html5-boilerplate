Our Mission

We're not here to help "pick up artists", players, or playettes. There's plenty of that on Tinder, and we're sure they have no trouble meeting people. What we have noticed is that (1) not everyone knows how to flirt in this iPhone addicted age, and (2) not everyone has good manners when it comes to dating. Wing is about demonstrating what we consider as both attractive and respectful conversation through (mostly) real online dating chats. Ever wonder how to gracefully navigate topics like "what are you looking for?" or "I'm in an open relationship." We got you. Curious about how to how to have conversation that increases someone's pulse rate (unlike work, weather, hobbies), while not being a creep? Let's play. We hope you enjoy Wing as much as we've enjoyed creating it. Holler at us with feedback. We love you.
